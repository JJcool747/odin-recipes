Butter Chicken Masala Recipe

Photo by:Kurman Communications License CC

Description

A tasty butter chicken masala plated on a bowl of rice. Simple and easy to cook while providing good nutrients.

Ingredients

  • 1 piece of Ginger
  • 150g of Basmati rice
  • 280g of diced chicken thigh
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • Spoonful of tomato puree
  • Small pot of Garam masala
  • 150ml of water
  • 1 cube of chicken stock
  • 150g of sour cream
  • 30g of butter

Steps

    • Boil a large pan of water and add 1/4 tablespoon of salt.
    • Peel the ginger and halve it.
    • Once the pan is boiling add the rice and half the ginger.
    • Cook for 12 minutes before draining with a sieve and placing it back into the pan covered by a lid.

    • In the meantime, heat some oil in a frying pan on medium heat.
    • After it's heated, add the chicken and season with salt and pepper before frying until golden brown.
    • As the chicken cooks, grate the other half of ginger as well as pressing the garlic.

    • After the ckicken has broned, add the puree, masal, grated ginger and half of the garlic.
    • Mix together,then pour in the water and add in the chicken stock ground up in your hands.
    • Bring to boiling and cook unitl reduced by half.

    • After the sauce is reduced to half, mix in the sour cream.
    • Bring just to boiling, then mix in the butter until fully melted then remove from heat.
    • Check the taste and add salt and pepper where necessary.

    • Remove the ginger from the rice and serve the rice.
    • Top the rice with the curry and enjoy the efforts of your labor.
