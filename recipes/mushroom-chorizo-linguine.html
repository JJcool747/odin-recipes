Mushroom and Chorizo Linguine Recipe

Photo by: Daniela Tentis

Description

A linguine dish with a rich tomato sauce topped with diced chorizo and a rich tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • 180g of Linguine
  • 1 Garlic clove
  • 60g of diced chorizo
  • 1 carton of tomato passata
  • 10g of vegetable stock paste
  • 25g of sun-dried tomato paste
  • 20g of grated hard Italian cheese
  • 50ml of water

Steps

    • Boil a large pan of water and add 1/4 tablespoon of salt.
    • Add the linguine to the pan and cook until tender.
    • Once cooked, drain through a colander and put back in the pan.
    • Drizzle with oil to stop the pasta sticking.

    • In the meantime, peal and crush the garlic using a press.
    • Heat a bit of oil in a large frying pan on medium temp.

    • Once the oil is hot add the chorizo and fry until golden.
    • Add the garlic and stir-fry for 30 seconds before mixing in the passata, both pastes and the water.
    • Add some salt and pepper before bringing to boiling and cook until thickened.

    • After the sauce is thickened, combine with the pasta in the largest pan.
    • Mix in half the Italian cheese and add some water if too thick.
    • Season with salt and pepper where necessary.

    • Serve onto your plate or bowl and add the remaining cheese on top.
